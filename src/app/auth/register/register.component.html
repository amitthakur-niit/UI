Before you start we want to let you know:

  • we are regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority
  • you will not be charged a fee for this service. However you may be required to pay other fees as part of your mortgage application such as product or valuation fees. Details of these will be provided before you confirm your mortgage product
  • we offer other borrowing options, such as overdrafts, credit cards and personal loans which may be more suitable depending on your circumstances

Applying online without advice

By applying online you won’t receive any advice from us. If you’d like advice, you’ll have the option to choose it after you’ve completed your online decision in principle.
